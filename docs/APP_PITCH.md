# Evidence Guardian — Product Pitch

## One-line pitch
**Evidence Guardian is a forensic-ready evidence vault for parents and families that turns everyday incidents into court-usable, chronologically ordered records.**

## The problem
People in family law, parenting, and domestic conflict matters are often told to “keep records,” but most records are:
- scattered across phones, messages, screenshots, receipts, emails, and apps,
- easy to challenge (“edited”, “missing context”, “wrong date”), and
- hard to present clearly in legal or support settings.

When evidence is disorganized, important patterns are missed and legal outcomes can be affected.

## The solution
Evidence Guardian gives users a secure, structured way to capture and upload evidence, then automatically preserves forensic context so records are more reliable and easier to present.

The app combines:
- **capture + upload** in one workflow,
- **forensic hardening** (hashing, trusted timestamping, device/location context), and
- **chronological evidence organization** for clearer narratives.

## Core value proposition
Evidence Guardian helps users move from **“I have files”** to **“I have a defensible timeline.”**

## Key capabilities
- **Evidence capture:** photo, video, and audio capture inside the app.
- **Broad evidence upload:** import additional materials such as:
  - photos,
  - video,
  - audio,
  - parenting app logs,
  - receipts,
  - correspondence,
  - and other supporting files.
- **Forensic integrity controls:**
  - SHA-256 hashing,
  - UTC timestamping,
  - device attribution,
  - available location metadata,
  - integrity verification checks.
- **Metadata-aware imports:** captures available source metadata (e.g., file/source timestamps and file details) and associates it with the evidence record.
- **Chronological ordering:** all evidence can be viewed as a time-ordered sequence to help show pattern and context.
- **Court workflow support:** court order linking, breach logging, and report/export pathways.

## Who it is for
- Parents managing high-conflict co-parenting records.
- Individuals documenting family law or protective-order related events.
- Advocates and legal support professionals helping clients preserve records.

## Why it matters
Evidence Guardian reduces the friction between documenting an incident and presenting it in a credible way. It helps users:
- protect original context,
- reduce disputes about authenticity,
- prepare faster for legal meetings, and
- communicate events as a coherent timeline rather than isolated files.

## Positioning statement
**Evidence Guardian is the trusted personal evidence timeline for family law and parenting disputes—built to preserve integrity from capture to court preparation.**

## Short spoken pitch (30 seconds)
“Evidence Guardian helps people in family and parenting disputes safely capture or upload evidence—photos, videos, audio, messages, receipts, and more—and automatically preserve forensic details like timestamps, hashes, and metadata. Instead of a messy folder of files, users get a clear chronological timeline they can use for legal, advocacy, and case preparation.”
